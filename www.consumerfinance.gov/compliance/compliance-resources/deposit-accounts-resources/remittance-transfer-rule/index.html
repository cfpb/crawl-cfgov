Remittance transfers

Resources to help industry participants understand, implement, and comply with the remittance transfer rule (subpart B of Regulation E).

Featured topic

On May 12, 2025, the CFPB withdrew several guidance documents, including “Statement regarding supervisory and enforcement practices in light of the COV1D-19 pandemic,” and “CFPB Bulletin 2012-08: Remittance transfer rule implementation.” You can read more about the withdrawal here .

On September 20, 2024, the Bureau proposed a narrowly tailored amendment to certain remittance transfer disclosure requirements and accompanying model forms under Regulation E.

Rules

Browse remittance transfer rule to see specific amendments to Regulation E.

Browse final rule defining larger participants in the international money transfer market.

Regulations and official interpretations

Browse the Remittance Rule (Subpart B of Regulation E, 12 CFR 1005) on:
 Interactive Bureau Regulations | eCFR

Main remittance transfer rule provisions and official interpretations can be found in:

  • § 1005.30, Definitions
  • § 1005.31, Disclosures
  • § 1005.32, Estimates
  • § 1005.33, Error resolution
  • § 1005.34, Cancellation and refund of remittance transfers
  • § 1005.35, Acts of agents
  • § 1005.36, Transfers scheduled before date of transfer
  • Appendix A, Model disclosure clauses and forms
